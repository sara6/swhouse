Southwest Surfhouse

Southwest Surf House (formally known as Carwyn Williams Surf House) was set up 16 years ago by ex professional Welsh surfer Carwyn Williams and now remains one of the longest running Surf Hostels in Europe. The Surfhouse is located just outside the famous surfing town of Hossegor in the beautiful woodlands of Seignosse. Over the years Carwyn has redefined what a Surfhouse can offer creating a relaxed atmosphere with guaranteed fun. Here at Southwest Surfhouse we strive on living the good life and having the most fun in and out of the ocean.

  • Surfing

    The southwest of France is renound for having the most sought after and consistant beach breaks in Europe, if not the world. Everyday our staff assess the surf conditions and plan around our guests surfing abilities. We drive you to the best surf spots in and around the Hossegor area everyday and will surf with you and offer any guidance necessary, to ensure you catch as many waves as possible.

  • Good Times

    Want to party? No problem, we will take you to the best bars/ parties available, even if that means a trip to Spain! Most people will find the best bar in Hossegor is our very own pool bar, but we will let you decide. Carwyn has dedicated his life to surfing, having fun and of course his Surfhouse. With over 30 years of combined local knowledge, let us show you how much fun can be had in the beautiful part of the world we call home.

  • The House

    The Surfhouse has 4 bed rooms, 6 bed rooms private doubles, and twin doubles. Outside there is a large entertaining area with a swimming pool, BBQ, table tennis, hammocks, slack lines, a fire pit, and our very own bar. The Surf Hostel is also equipped with free WIFI, Sky TV, endless books/DVDs/magazines, wetsuits, and skate/longboards all available for you to use.

Yoga

Yoga and Surfing are a match made in heaven. Our 7 day retreat is based around nourishing and revitalising the body and mind through energising yoga, serene surfing, delicious food and great company.

The week long retreat is suited to Yogis of all levels. Our expert teacher Issy welcomes beginners and also accommodates intermediate/advanced yogis a chance to deepen there practice. Each morning we start the day with an invigorating vinyasa flow (75 minutes) to get everyone warmed up and ready for surfing. Evening classes are restorative and yin based to help restore the body and mind after the days surf. One evening class will also be held on the beach at sunset! No classes are obligatory but we offer the chance to practice 2.5 hours of yoga per day.
